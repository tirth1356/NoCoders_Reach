# Reach  
Social Media Growth Platform for Bharat  

---

## 1. Product Overview

Reach is a unified digital content growth platform designed for creators, startups, and small businesses in India.

It enables users to create, optimize, schedule, and analyze social media content across platforms — with built-in multilingual support for Indian regional languages.

The platform simplifies content workflows while improving engagement and visibility.

## 3. Problem Context

Digital creators struggle with:

- Managing content across multiple platforms  
- Limited support for Indian regional languages  
- Guesswork-based posting without insights  
- Fragmented scheduling and analytics tools  
- Expensive AI-based growth solutions  

These issues reduce consistency, reach, and growth potential.

---

## 4. Proposed Solution

Reach provides a centralized AI-assisted system that:

- Simplifies content creation  
- Supports multilingual publishing  
- Improves content before posting  
- Enables structured scheduling  
- Provides data-driven performance insights  

The solution focuses on accessibility, affordability, and intelligent automation.

---

## 5. Core Objectives

- AI-assisted content creation  
- Multilingual content support  
- Engagement scoring and optimization  
- Smart scheduling recommendations  
- Centralized analytics dashboard  
- Short-form video script generation  

---

## 6. Functional Requirements

### 6.1 User Management
- Users must register and log in securely.
- Users must manage profile details.
- Users must access a personalized dashboard.



### 6.2 Content Creation
- Generate content for:
  - Instagram  
  - LinkedIn  
  - Twitter/X  
  - Blog  

- Users must input:
  - Topic  
  - Audience  
  - Tone  
  - Keywords  

- System must:
  - Generate variations  
  - Suggest hashtags  
  - Suggest call-to-action lines  



### 6.3 Multilingual Support
- Support at least 5 Indian regional languages.
- Allow content translation.
- Maintain tone and context consistency.



### 6.4 Content Optimization
- Analyze sentiment.
- Generate engagement score (0–100).
- Extract keywords.
- Provide improvement suggestions.



### 6.5 Scheduling
- Calendar-based scheduling.
- Edit and delete scheduled posts.
- Track draft and publish status.
- Recommend optimal posting times.



### 6.6 Analytics
- Display engagement trends.
- Highlight top-performing content.
- Show keyword impact.
- Provide AI-driven recommendations.



### 6.7 Video Script Generation
- Generate structured short-form scripts.
- Provide scene breakdown.
- Create voiceover-ready text.

---

## 7. Non-Functional Requirements

- Responsive design (mobile + desktop).
- Fast content generation response time.
- Secure authentication and data handling.
- Support concurrent users.
- Cost-efficient deployment.

---

## 8. Constraints

- Limited hackathon timeline.
- External social publishing may be simulated.
- Limited real-time analytics data during demo.
- Budget-conscious infrastructure.

---

## 9. Success Criteria

- End-to-end workflow functions without failure.
- Multilingual generation works for at least 5 languages.
- Optimization scoring is visible and accurate.
- Scheduling stores and retrieves data correctly.
- Analytics provide meaningful insights.
